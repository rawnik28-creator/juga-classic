FAQ

Answers to common questions about the Skill Driver game.

How do I play the game?

Use the 'W, A, S, D' or arrow keys to control your car. Dodge obstacles, collect coins, and survive as long as you can to get the highest score!

Is the game free to play?

Yes, Skill Driver is completely free to play in your browser. There are no downloads or installations required.

How is my high score saved?

Your high score is saved automatically in your browser's local storage. It's private to you and will be there the next time you play on the same device.

Can I play on my mobile phone?

Absolutely! The game is designed to be fully responsive and works great on both desktop and mobile devices.

Are you planning to add new features?

Yes! We are always working on new cars, levels, and game modes. Check out our Dev Blog for the latest news and updates.
