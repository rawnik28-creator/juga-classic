Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Overview

AutoTech NZ/CA is an informational website about new car technologies in New Zealand and Canada. We do not require account signup and we minimize data collection.

Information We Handle

  • Local data: any simulation scores or preferences are stored in your browser’s localStorage, never sent to our servers.
  • Contact messages: if you choose to contact us, we receive the information you submit.

Cookies

AutoTech NZ/CA does not set cookies by default. Any third-party analytics tools would operate under their own policies.

Your Choices

  • Clear your browser's site data to remove any locally stored information.
  • Use private browsing to avoid storing local data.

Contact

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Overview

Skill Driver is a browser-based game. We respect your privacy, do not require account signup, and minimize data collection.

Information We Handle

  • Local data: your high scores and game preferences are stored in your browser’s localStorage. This data is not sent to our servers.
  • Contact messages: if you choose to contact us via our form, we receive the information you submit to respond to your query.

Cookies

Skill Driver does not use cookies for tracking. Any third-party analytics tools would operate under their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser's site data at any time to remove any locally stored high scores or settings.
  • Use your browser's private or incognito mode to play without storing any data locally.

Contact

Questions about our game or this policy? Use the contact form on the Home page.
