Sustainable Materials in NZ/CA Car Manufacturing

Beyond the powertrain, the future of cars in NZ/CA is green.

Published 2025-08-22

Automakers are innovating with materials that reduce environmental impact for NZ/CA models without compromising quality or safety.

Recycled Composites

Using recycled plastics and fibers to create durable, lightweight interior and exterior components for NZ/CA vehicles.

Vegan Interiors

High-quality, cruelty-free materials like plant-based leather and textiles are becoming the new standard for luxury vehicles in NZ/CA.

Eco-Friendly Production

Reducing water usage, waste, and carbon footprint in the manufacturing process for NZ/CA markets.
